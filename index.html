A Legacy of Perfection

The Dolphins stand alone in NFL history, achieving a feat no other team has ever matched. This legacy of greatness is the foundation of a proud franchise.

The 1972 Perfect Season

The only team in NFL history to complete an entire season, including the Super Bowl, undefeated and untied. We're looking at you, '07 Patriots.

They followed this up with another championship in 1973, cementing a dynasty with 5 total Super Bowl appearances.

Titans of Miami: Legendary Players

The Dolphins' history is rich with Hall of Fame talent who didn't just play the game‚Äîthey defined their positions.

Dan Marino

  • Record-Shattering QB: Threw for a then-record 5,084 yards and 48 TDs in 1984.
  • Career Powerhouse: Retired with over 61,000 passing yards and 420 touchdowns.
  • The Standard: Redefined the quarterback position with his lightning-quick release and cannon arm.

Larry Csonka

  • The Perfect Engine: A bruising fullback and the heart of the '72 Perfect Season offense.
  • Super Bowl MVP: Dominated Super Bowl VIII with 145 yards and 2 TDs.
  • Mr. Consistent: Rushed for over 1,000 yards in three consecutive seasons, all leading to Super Bowl appearances.

Jason Taylor

  • Defensive Player of the Year: Won the award in 2006 with a stunning all-around season.
  • Sack King: The Dolphins' all-time leader with 131 sacks.
  • Touchdown Machine: Scored 9 defensive touchdowns in his career, an NFL record for a defensive lineman.
